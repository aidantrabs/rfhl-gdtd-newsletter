Who We Are

If you have ever used the app, applied for a loan online, checked your portfolio, or walked into a branch and watched something work seamlessly on screen - that was us.

We are the Group Digital Technology Division - the team that designs, builds, and operates the digital platforms powering Republic Financial across every territory.

From payments and cards to digital onboarding, lending, wealth management, and the SuperApp - we own the platforms that connect our customers to the Group.
